Brownies

Description

Decadent fudgy brownies rich with chocolate flavor that will absolutely melt in your mouth. The easiest and tastiest brownies you'll ever make!

Ingredients

  • 200 gm unsweetened dark chocolate bar
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, Cut your chocolate into little chunks, and melt it together with the butter in a double boiler setup. Once it's melted, set it aside t cool down for abit while you start with the dry ingredients.
  2. In a large bowl, put your white and brown sugars together, and crack one egg and whisk it in one by one, untill all the sugar is creamed.
  3. Once the sugar is creamed and mixed well with the eggs, add the vanilla extract.
  4. While mixing, pour in the chocolate and butter mixture into the sugar and eggs. make sure the chocolate isn't too hot or it might cook the eggs.
  5. Once properly incorporated, sift in the flour and cocoa powder into your wet mix.
  6. Slowly fold the flour and cocoa into your batter. DO NOT OVER MIX, or too much air will get into the batter and the brownie might end up cakey
  7. Pour the batter into a 7x7 inch pan, and put the pan into a preheated oven at 180 Â°C for 25-30 minutes. At around 25 minutes, check the brownie with a toothpick. It should come out slightly chocolatey but not too much and not completely clean.
  8. Let cool off for a few hours before cutting into sqaures pieces.
  9. Enjoy!
